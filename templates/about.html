Push Chess Forward.

ChessAI isn't just another engine wrapper. It's a new way to understand the game, focusing on the human elements of chess—intuition, pressure, and harmony.

Our Mission

Traditional engines give you a number: +0.5. But they don't tell you why. Is it because you have more space? Because your pieces are better coordinated? Or because your opponent made a subtle strategic error?

ChessAI breaks down that single number into 20+ qualitative metrics. We quantify abstract concepts like "Harmony" and "Resourcefulness" so you can train specific aspects of your game that raw calculation can't teach.

The Technology

Graph Theory

Our "Harmony" and "Connectivity" metrics use graph theory algorithms to model the defensive web of your pieces.

Python Core

Bitboard Logic
