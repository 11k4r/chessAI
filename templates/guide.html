Metric Guide

Understanding the 20+ advanced metrics behind ChessAI.

Player Performance

Intuition

Measures accuracy in "low-complexity" positions. High intuition means you find natural, good moves easily without needing deep calculation.

Tactics

Accuracy in sharp, critical positions (high complexity). High tactics score means you excel in chaotic, calculation-heavy moments.

Resourcefulness

Your ability to find the best defense in difficult or losing positions. It measures tenacity under pressure.

Pressure Handling

Accuracy specifically when your King Safety score is low (i.e., your king is under attack).

Board Dynamics

Harmony

A measure of piece coordination. Calculated by how many of your pieces are protected by other friendly pieces.

Connectivity

Total number of defensive links between your pieces. A highly connected army is harder to double-attack.

Space

The number of squares you control in the opponent's half of the board.

Mobility

The raw number of legal moves available to you. More mobility generally means more options and activity.

Game Flow

Volatility

Standard deviation of the evaluation graph. High volatility means a wild game with big swings; low means a steady, controlled game.

Complexity

A heuristic score (0-10) estimating how hard a position is to calculate, based on piece tension, open lines, and king safety.

Criticality

Measures how "forced" the current moment is. High criticality means there is likely only one good move (e.g., check evasion).

Novelty / Commonality

Estimates how often the current position appears in opening theory. 0% is a completely new position (Novelty).
