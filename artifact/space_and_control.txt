# Feature Extraction Framework: Level 1 - Position Level (The State)
## Category: Space and Control

**Description:** This category measures the amount of physical territory dictated by each player. It translates pawn advancement and piece influence into a quantifiable measure of "breathing room" and central dominance, tracking how a player restricts the opponent's maneuvering capabilities.

### Extracted Features

* **Center_Control_Index:**
  - Description: The total number of attacks generated by a player's pieces and pawns on the four central squares (d4, d5, e4, e5). Redundant attackers on the same square increase the score, representing fortified central dominance.
  - Data Type: Integer (per player)

* **Pawn_Center_Presence:**
  - Description: The number of friendly pawns physically occupying the four central squares (d4, d5, e4, e5) or the expanded center (c4, c5, f4, f5).
  - Data Type: Integer (per player)

* **Enemy_Territory_Control:**
  - Description: The total count of squares in the opponent's half of the board (Ranks 5-8 for White; Ranks 1-4 for Black) that are directly attacked or defended by friendly pieces.
  - Data Type: Integer (per player)

* **Space_Advantage_Metric:**
  - Description: A spatial measurement defined by the number of safe squares located *behind* a player's advanced pawn structure. Higher pawn chains naturally grant higher space metrics.
  - Data Type: Integer (per player)

* **Flank_Domination_Balance:**
  - Description: Calculates the ratio or differential of controlled squares on the Kingside (files f, g, h) versus the Queenside (files a, b, c) to detect where a player's territorial pressure is concentrated.
  - Data Type: String / Dictionary (e.g., {"White": "Kingside +4", "Black": "Queenside +2"})

* **Contested_Squares_Density:**
  - Description: The total number of squares on the board that are simultaneously attacked by at least one White piece and at least one Black piece. High values indicate immense positional tension and fights for critical outposts.
  - Data Type: Integer (global board state)
