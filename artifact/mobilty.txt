# Feature Extraction Framework: Level 1 - Position Level (The State)
## Category: Piece Activity / Mobility

**Description:** This category measures the range, placement quality, and movement freedom of pieces on an individual basis. It translates raw material into functional power.

### Extracted Features

* **Safe_Mobility_Score:**
  - Description: The aggregate count of legal moves available to a player's pieces that do not land on squares dominated by lower-value enemy pieces.
  - Data Type: Integer / Float (aggregate or per-piece-type)

* **Trapped_Piece_Count:**
  - Description: The number of active major or minor pieces that have 0 or 1 safe legal moves available, indicating severe restriction.
  - Data Type: Integer (per player)

* **Bishop_Scope:**
  - Description: The ratio or raw count of unobstructed squares available to a bishop along its diagonals. Differentiates between Good and Bad Bishops.
  - Data Type: Float (average squares per bishop)

* **Advanced_Rooks:**
  - Description: The number of rooks that have successfully penetrated to the 7th or 8th rank (2nd or 1st for Black).
  - Data Type: Integer (0-2 per player)

* **Piece_Centralization_Index:**
  - Description: The aggregate Manhattan distance of all minor and major pieces from the four central squares (d4, d5, e4, e5).
  - Data Type: Float / Integer

* **Development_Status:**
  - Description: A count of minor and major pieces that remain on their starting squares.
  - Data Type: Integer (undeployed pieces count)
