# Feature Extraction Framework: Level 1 - Position Level (The State)
## Category: King Safety

**Description:** This category strictly measures the localized defensive perimeter around both kings and their vulnerability to direct attack. It isolates the physical safety of the king's immediate environment from broader board activity.

### Extracted Features

* **Pawn_Shield_Integrity:**
  - Description: The structural health of the friendly pawns directly shielding the king (e.g., the f, g, and h pawns for a kingside castle). Missing or overextended pawns represent a lower integrity score.
  - Data Type: Categorical / Integer (e.g., Intact, Compromised, Destroyed)

* **Open_Files_On_King:**
  - Description: The number of open or semi-open files that run directly adjacent to or directly through the king's current position.
  - Data Type: Integer (0-3)

* **King_Ring_Attackers:**
  - Description: The total number of active enemy pieces whose attack rays directly intersect with the 3x3 grid (the King Ring) immediately surrounding the king.
  - Data Type: Integer (per player)

* **King_Ring_Defenders:**
  - Description: The total number of friendly pieces actively defending squares within the 3x3 King Ring, representing the size of the defensive garrison.
  - Data Type: Integer (per player)

* **King_Xray_Threats:**
  - Description: The number of enemy sliding pieces (Queens, Rooks, Bishops) aligned with the king on a rank, file, or diagonal, but currently obstructed by one or more pieces. Flags absolute pins and latent discovered attacks.
  - Data Type: Integer (per player)

* **Escape_Squares_Luft:**
  - Description: The number of safe, legal moves immediately available to the king. A value of 0 flags high susceptibility to sudden mating nets (e.g., back-rank mates).
  - Data Type: Integer (per player)

* **Pawn_Storm_Proximity:**
  - Description: An indicator tracking the advancement of enemy pawns on the specific flank where the king is located.
  - Data Type: Boolean / Integer (tracking pawn depth)

* **King_Centralization_Exposure:**
  - Description: The distance of the king from the center (d4, d5, e4, e5). This represents a severe liability in the middlegame but becomes a critical asset in the endgame.
  - Data Type: Integer (Manhattan distance to center)
